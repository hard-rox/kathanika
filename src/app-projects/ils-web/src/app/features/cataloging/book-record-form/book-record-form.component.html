MARC21 Bibliographic Record Structure:

  • Title (245$a): Title proper only, without statement of responsibility
  • ISBN (020$a): International Standard Book Number without hyphens
  • Author (100$a): Main entry personal name in inverted form (Surname, Forename)
  • Publisher (260/264$b): Name of publisher
  • Date (260/264$c): Date of publication (YYYY)
  • Extent (300$a): Number and specific material designation (325 pages)
  • Dimensions (300$c): Dimensions (23 cm)
